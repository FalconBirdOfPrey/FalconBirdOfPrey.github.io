You have come to the right place, here is where I display various personal projects!

This, my first project, is a manuscript written in Swedish, of a book which is more of a novella than a novel. On the surface a story about industrial espionage as a consequence of incompetent, destructive and dangerous leadership. Acutely relevant in today's society. Although the narrative is rooted in reality, it is to be read as a fictional chronicle.
